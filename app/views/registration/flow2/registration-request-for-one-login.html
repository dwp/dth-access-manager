Submit a registration request for GOV.UK One Login

You can use this service to:

  • Submit an integration request to DWP Access Manager production environment.

This will enable you to connect up to GOV.UK One Login.

It takes around 5 minutes to register. You will be able to check your answers before submitting.

The Identity and Trust Service Management Team will handle your request and update you on your service status.

Before you start

It is essential that you have all the information you need to hand before you register a request.

You will need the following:

  • Your personal details
  • Line manager email address
  • Approver's email address
  • Your DWP service name
  • Your Teams Slack channel
  • Possibly your Public key details
  • Pass redirect URL
  • Failure redirect URL
  • Logout URL
  • Scopes
  • Claims

If you need help using this service

Please see our support page for further guidance.

You can email the team directly on dthclient.itsm@DWP.gov.uk

We’ll reply by email within 2 days.

We’ll respond during office hours: Monday to Friday, Office hours are 8am to 6pm.
