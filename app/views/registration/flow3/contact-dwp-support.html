Contact DWP Support Management Team

Before you contact us

  • Make sure you have had training on how to use the service.
  • Where applicable read the service start page for journey guidance.
  • Always check you have all the information needed before attempting to submit or update a registration request.
  • See if a member of your team can provide additional help with the service.

The Identity and Trust Service Management Team will handle any requests and update on integration status.

If you need help using this service

If you're having trouble using any part of the DWP Access Manager service, you can get help from our Management Support Team.

Ways to contact us

Email

You can email the team directly on dthclient.itsm@DWP.gov.uk

We’ll reply by email within 2 days.

We’ll respond during office hours: Monday to Friday, 9:30am to 5:30pm

How we use your information

To find out what information we store when you contact us and how we use it, see the DWP privacy notice.
